This ratatouille is not only super flavorful and beautiful, but easy to make. The bulk of the work lies in chopping each of the vegetables and then layering them in whatever shape you like. I prefer circular because it gives it a little something extra compared to a square pan, but feel free to use whatever is in your kitchen!

 

Ratatouille
Recipe Type: Dinner
Author: Meagan DiVito
Prep time: 20 mins
Cook time: 45 mins
Total time: 1 hour 5 mins
Serves: 4
Ingredients
  • 1 cup spaghetti sauce
  • 1/2 yellow onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 eggplant, (small if possible) cut in 1/4" slices
  • 1 tomato, cut in 1/4" slices
  • 1 zucchini, cut in 1/4" slices
  • 1 summer squash, cut in 1/4" slices
  • 1 pepper, any color, cut in 1/4" slices
  • 1 Tbs EVOO
  • 1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp Dried Basil
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, wash and prep vegetables.
  2. Mix spaghetti sauce, onion, and garlic in bottom of 8 inch round pan.
  3. Layer eggplant, tomato, zucchini, summer squash, and pepper around the pan. Once around, fill the middle of the circle with 1 layer of vegetables.
  4. Drizzle olive oil then spices over vegetables.
  5. Cover with tinfoil and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove cover and cook for an additional 15 minutes.
  7. Serve over your favorite pasta, rice, or couscous.
